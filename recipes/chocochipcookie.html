Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are my absolute favorite type of cookie. I like making my chocolate chip cookies using Honeysuckle's recipe. They have the perfect amount of sweetness and have a great ratio of cripsy outside and chewy inside. Her recipe is also very simple to recreate. Below is her recipe.

Time

    45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 8 tbsp of butter (Kerrygold butter is recommended)
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1/2 tsp salt (kosher)
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 1/3 cups AP flour
  • 1 cup large chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Melt and carefully brown 8 tbsp high-quality butter.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mix in your white sugar, brown sugar, egg, vanilla, salt, and baking soda.
  3. Then once it’s a ribbon-like consistency, add the flour and chocolate chips.
  4. Mix well, scoop into round dough balls on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Sprinkle with finishing salt if desired. Chill 10 minutes if you’d like and bake for 10-12 minutes at 350º F.
  5. Cool and serve.
  6. Enjoy!
