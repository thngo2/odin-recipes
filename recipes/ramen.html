Ponyo Ramen Recipe

Inspired by the heartwarming meal from Ponyo, this simple ramen is easy to make and comforting to eat!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of instant ramen
  • 1 egg
  • Slice of ham
  • Chopped green onions (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the ramen.
    • Boil water in a pot and cook the instant ramen noodles according to the package instructions.
  2. Add the egg.
    • Once the noodles are almost cooked, crack an egg into the pot. Let it cook for 2-3 minutes until the egg is poached to your liking.
  3. Add the slice of ham.
    • While the egg is cooking, add a slice of ham to the pot to warm it up.
  4. Pour the ramen into a bowl, making sure the egg and ham are on top. Garnish with green onions if desired.
