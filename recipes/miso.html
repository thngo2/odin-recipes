Miso Soup

Miso soup is one of my favorite soups. It's great for your health, delicious, and simple to make. The measurements aren't exact but here is how I make my miso soup.

Time

    10 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of miso paste
  • Tofu (optional)
  • Green onions (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil your water until it's bubbling.
  2. Add in your tofu.
  3. Once the water is bubbling hot, take the pot off of the stove.
  4. Take your miso paste and mix it in until it dissolves.
  5. Once the paste dissolves, add in your green onions.
  6. Enjoy!
