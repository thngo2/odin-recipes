Miso Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of miso paste
  • Tofu (optional)
  • Green onions (optional)

Instructions

  1. Bring your water to a boil in a pot.
  2. Add in your tofu.
  3. Once the water is bubbling hot, take the pot off of the stove.
  4. Take your miso paste and mix it in until it dissolves.
  5. Once the paste is dissolved, add in your green onions.
  6. Enjoy!
