Howl's Moving Castle Bacon and Eggs Recipe

Bring a touch of magic to your morning with a bacon and eggs recipe inspired by Howl's Moving Castle. Just like in the film, enjoy a hearty, simple breakfast that's as satisfying as it is delicious. Let your kitchen feel as cozy as Calcifer's hearth!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 2 large eggs
  • Optional seasonings: chili flakes, salt, and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the bacon.
    • Heat a skillet over medium heat.
    • Place the bacon slices in the skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes per side, or until crispy. Remove and set aside on a paper towel to drain.
  2. Cook the eggs.
    • Using the same skillet (with bacon grease), crack the eggs directly into the pan.
    • Cook sunny side up for 2-3 minutes until the whites are set, or flip for over-easy.
    • Season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes to taste (optional).
  3. Plate the bacon and eggs together, and enjoy a savory start to your day. Enjoy!
